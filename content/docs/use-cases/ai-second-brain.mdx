# Build an AI-Powered Second Brain

The "second brain" concept is simple: offload your memory to a trusted external system. Naidis makes your second brain intelligent.

## What Is a Second Brain?

A second brain is a personal knowledge management (PKM) system that:

- **Captures** ideas, notes, and information
- **Organizes** content for future retrieval
- **Distills** insights for understanding
- **Expresses** knowledge through creation

The problem? Traditional second brains are passive. You put information in, but getting it out requires remembering where you put it.

## Building Your AI Second Brain

### Step 1: Set Up the Foundation

1. Install Obsidian
2. Install Naidis
3. Create your folder structure:

```
/Inbox          - New captures land here
/Projects       - Active work
/Areas          - Ongoing responsibilities
/Resources      - Topic-based reference
/Archive        - Completed or inactive
```

### Step 2: Capture Aggressively

Don't filter upfront. Capture anything potentially useful:

- Interesting article? Clip it.
- Good YouTube video? Transcript it.
- Reading a book? Import highlights.
- Found a PDF? Extract it.

Your AI can search everything. Capture first, organize later.

### Step 3: Process Regularly

Weekly processing session:

1. Review inbox items
2. Add links and tags
3. Move to appropriate folders
4. Identify key insights for flashcards

### Step 4: Converse Daily

Make AI chat part of your workflow:

- Morning: "What should I review from yesterday's captures?"
- Working: "What do my notes say about [current problem]?"
- Evening: "Summarize what I learned today"

The AI becomes your thinking partner.

### Step 5: Review Consistently

Daily spaced repetition (5-10 minutes):

- Review due flashcards
- Maintain your streak
- Reinforce key knowledge
