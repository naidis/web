## Detailed Comparison

### 1. Naidis - Best All-in-One

**What it does**: AI chat + YouTube transcripts + web clipping + PDF processing + Kindle sync + spaced repetition + RSS reader.

**Pricing**: $29 one-time

**Pros**:
- Most comprehensive feature set
- One-time payment (no subscription)
- Open source core (Rust)
- Local-first (privacy focused)

**Cons**:
- Less polished AI chat than Copilot
- Newer plugin, smaller community

**Best for**: Users who want a complete PKM toolkit with AI, not just chat.

### 2. Obsidian Copilot - Best AI Chat

**What it does**: AI-powered chat for your vault with RAG, multiple LLM support, and exclusive models.

**Pricing**: $14.99/month, $139.99/year, or $299 lifetime

**Pros**:
- Most polished AI chat experience
- Exclusive copilot-plus models
- Projects mode for organized conversations
- Well-established, active development

**Cons**:
- Expensive ($299 lifetime or ongoing subscription)
- Only does AI chat (no content capture)
- Closed source

**Best for**: Users who only need AI chat and want the best experience.

### 3. Smart Connections - Best Free Option

**What it does**: AI-powered similarity matching to surface related notes. Smart View shows connections in real-time.

**Pricing**: Free (optional $5-20/month supporter tiers)

**Pros**:
- Completely free for full features
- Excellent at discovering note relationships
- Active development and community
- Simple to set up

**Cons**:
- Limited to note discovery (no content capture)
- AI chat less developed than competitors

**Best for**: Users on a budget who want AI-powered note discovery.
