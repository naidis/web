# Spaced Repetition

Review your highlights daily with scientifically-proven spaced repetition algorithms. Never forget what you read.

## Overview

Naidis includes a complete spaced repetition system inspired by Readwise's Daily Review. It supports:

- **Highlight Review**: Revisit your book/article highlights at optimal intervals
- **Mastery Cards**: Create Q&A and cloze deletion flashcards from highlights
- **Streak Tracking**: Build a daily review habit with streak stats
- **Two Algorithms**: Choose between SM-2 (SuperMemo) and Half-life Decay

## Quick Start

1. Open the Command Palette with `Cmd+Shift+P`
2. Select "Daily Review" or press `Cmd+Shift+R`
3. Review your highlights and mastery cards
4. Build your streak!

## Daily Review Flow

### Highlight Actions

When reviewing a highlight, you can:

| Action | Shortcut | Effect |
|--------|----------|--------|
| **Keep** | `Enter` | Mark as reviewed, schedule next review |
| **Discard** | `D` | Remove from future reviews |
| **Master** | `M` | Create a Q&A or Cloze card |
| **Add Note** | `N` | Add a personal note |
| **Add Tag** | `T` | Tag the highlight |

### Mastery Card Actions

For Q&A and Cloze cards:

| Action | Shortcut | Effect |
|--------|----------|--------|
| **Show Answer** | `Space` | Reveal the answer |
| **Soon** | `1` | Review again soon (forgot) |
| **Later** | `2` | Review later (remembered with effort) |
| **Someday** | `3` | Review much later (easy recall) |
| **Never** | `0` | Remove from reviews |

### Navigation

- `←` Previous item
- `→` Next item

## Algorithms

### SM-2 (SuperMemo)

The classic spaced repetition algorithm used by Anki and SuperMemo. Proven effective over decades.

- Predictable intervals: 1 day → 6 days → 15 days → etc.
- Adjusts based on recall quality
- Best for: Consistent, predictable review schedules

### Half-life Decay

A newer, adaptive algorithm that models memory as exponential decay.

- Calculates recall probability in real-time
- Adapts faster to individual learning patterns
- Best for: Adaptive scheduling, variable study patterns

## Mastery Cards

Turn highlights into flashcards for deeper retention.

### Q&A Cards

Create question-answer pairs from your highlights:

```
Highlight: "The mitochondria is the powerhouse of the cell"

Question: What is the powerhouse of the cell?
Answer: The mitochondria
```

### Cloze Deletions

Hide parts of text to test recall:

```
Original: The mitochondria is the powerhouse of the cell
Cloze: The {{mitochondria}} is the powerhouse of the cell

During review: The [...] is the powerhouse of the cell
```

Use `{{...}}` syntax to mark hidden text.

## Statistics

View your review statistics:

- **Current Streak**: Consecutive days reviewed
- **Longest Streak**: Your personal best
- **Today/Week/Total**: Review counts
- **Streak Recovery**: Miss a day? You can recover within 24 hours

Access stats via Command Palette → "View Statistics"

## Settings

Configure in Settings → Review tab:

| Setting | Default | Description |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Algorithm | SM-2 | Choose SM-2 or Half-life Decay |
| Highlights per day | 15 | Max highlights to review daily |
| Mastery cards per day | 10 | Max cards to review daily |

## Frequency Tuning

Adjust review frequency per document or source type:

- **Documents**: Review some books more/less frequently
- **Source Types**: Prioritize articles over tweets, etc.

Frequency multiplier: 0.5x (half as often) to 2.0x (twice as often)

## Tips

- **Start small**: Begin with 10-15 highlights per day
- **Be consistent**: Short daily sessions beat long irregular ones
- **Use Mastery for key facts**: Not every highlight needs a flashcard
- **Discard liberally**: Remove highlights that no longer matter
- **Check stats weekly**: Celebrate your streak!
