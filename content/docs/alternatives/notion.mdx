# Notion Alternative: Naidis

Looking for a **Notion alternative** that keeps your data local? Naidis provides powerful PKM features for Obsidian users who want privacy, offline access, and a one-time payment.

## Why Consider a Notion Alternative?

Notion is powerful, but has limitations for privacy-conscious users:

- **Cloud-only**: All your notes live on Notion's servers
- **$10-15/month**: Personal Pro costs $10/mo, Plus costs $15/mo
- **Internet required**: No offline editing without workarounds
- **Vendor lock-in**: Proprietary format, limited export options
- **AI add-on cost**: Notion AI is an extra $10/month

## What Naidis Does Better

### 1. True Local-First

Your notes stay on your computer as markdown files. No cloud uploads, no syncing delays, no outages affecting your work.

### 2. One-Time Payment

Pay $29 once. No monthly fees, no AI add-on charges, no price increases.

### 3. AI Without Extra Cost

Naidis includes AI chat with RAG (retrieval-augmented generation). Ask questions about your vault, get context-aware answers. Use your own API keys.

### 4. Content Capture Suite

Unlike Notion's basic web clipper, Naidis captures:
- YouTube video transcripts with timestamps
- Full web articles with clean formatting
- PDF text extraction and OCR
- Kindle highlights

### 5. Spaced Repetition

Built-in review system for highlights. Convert any highlight to a flashcard. Track streaks and mastery.
