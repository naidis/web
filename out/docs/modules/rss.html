RSS Reader Module

Subscribe to RSS/Atom feeds and save articles as Obsidian notes.

Usage

  1. Open the Command Palette with Cmd+Shift+P
  2. Select "RSS"
  3. Enter the RSS feed URL
  4. Click "Fetch"
  5. Click an article to save it

Features

Feed Parsing

Supports major feed formats like RSS 2.0 and Atom.

Article List

Displays the latest article list from the feed. Default is 20 items.

Individual Saving

Clicking an article saves it as a note. The entire content is converted to Markdown.

Note Template

Supported Feed Formats

  • RSS 2.0
  • RSS 1.0
  • Atom 1.0

Tips

  • Feed URLs are usually found at /feed, /rss, or /atom.xml on blogs
  • Some sites may provide only excerpts instead of full content
  • If the feed URL is not clear, look for <link rel="alternate" type="application/rss+xml"> in the site source

Spaced Repetition

Readwise-style daily review for highlight retention

PDF Processing

PDF text/table extraction and OCR
